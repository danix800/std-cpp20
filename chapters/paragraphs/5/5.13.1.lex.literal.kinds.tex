% 5.13.1 Kinds of literals [lex.literal.kinds]
\paragraph{} % 1
存在多种字面值。\footnote{本文件中术语“字面值”一般指\isoc{}中称为“常量”的符号。}

\synsym{literal}
  \synprd{\nt{integer-literal}}
  \synprd{\nt{character-literal}}
  \synprd{\nt{floating-point-literal}}
  \synprd{\nt{string-literal}}
  \synprd{\nt{boolean-literal}}
  \synprd{\nt{pointer-literal}}
  \synprd[]{\nt{user-defined-literal}}
